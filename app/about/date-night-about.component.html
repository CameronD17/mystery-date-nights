After moving into our current flat in September 2015, Sasha and I neglected our dating life in favour of working on redecorating and furnishing the flat.

Realising we needed to change, we made a pact to try two new date night places each month (I talk about this a little more in my blog post Local). This project, developed in order for me to learn Angular 2, chronicles where we've been on our date nights since then.

This app allows sorting and viewing of our date nights in both list view and map view.
